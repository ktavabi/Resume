I am excited to apply for the Data Engineer role at the City of Seattle's Data Engineering and Analytics team. The position's responsibilities and qualifications align well with my background, skills, and passion for data analytics, making me an ideal fit for the job.

As a Research Science Engineer at the Institute for Learning \& Brain Sciences (ILABS) at the University of Washington, I conducted cutting-edge research on early childhood learning and neurodevelopmental disorders in pediatric populations. I designed and executed experiments, analyzed data using statistical methods, and synthesized insights from medical imaging datasets. My work at the ILABS also involved:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Advising department leadership for data reporting activities \& facilitating strategic analytics planning.
  \item Presenting research findings at national and international conferences, authoring scientific publications in peer-reviewed journals, and managing grant applications.
  \item Collaboration with interdisciplinary researchers to design \& implement experimental paradigms for data engineering.
  \item Mentoring graduate students \& use expertise and analysis skills to assist other researchers in solving technical problems.  
\end{itemize}
These experiences have equipped me with the necessary skills to understand data operation requirements for business intelligence, develop ETL/ELT processes, and design data management schemas for the City of Seattle's Data Engineering and Analytics team.

I am a seasoned data professional with a Ph.D. in social sciences and over ten years of postdoctoral experience working with complex datasets. I am proficient in programming languages such as PYTHON, R, MATLAB, and SQL, and I have utilized these tools to perform DataOps, such as exploratory data analysis, mining, and visualization. My strong analytical skills and attention to detail make me proficient at working with different data types and monitoring data quality using various IT analytics products to support public safety data solutions, which are critical components of the Data Engineer role.

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated excellent communication skills, including written, verbal, and illustrative communication. I have effectively communicated technical content to professionals from diverse backgrounds, including business stakeholders, technical professionals, and management personnel. Moreover, I have experience engaging with team members and providing expertise and support for data science projects with reporting or analytics requirements.

I am committed to addressing race-based disparities and ending institutionalized racism, and I understand the importance of data in achieving this goal. As a Data Engineer, I will actively participate in the City of Seattle's race and social justice initiatives to support this need and create standard processes for using a related tool kit in all projects.

I look forward to bringing my expertise to the Data Engineering and Analytics team at the City of Seattle. Thank you for considering my application.

\end{cvletter}
